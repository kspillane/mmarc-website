WØO Event Announcement - October 31, 2025

WØO will once again be haunting the ether on Halloween from Frankenstein, MO. Stop by and say hi to Igor, Vlad, Drac or whoever else might awake to tickle the ionosphere. CW and SSB will be in play. Bewitching hour starts around 1800 UTC October 31st continues until the bands die. Watch the spotting networks for current frequencies.

To receive this year's unique QSL card you must send contact info along with a No. 10 SASE (smaller envelopes will require the card to be folded). See QRZ.com for details.

Operating Opportunities

MID-MO ARC NET

Every Wednesday night at 8:00pm is the Mid-MO Amateur Radio Club Net on 147.00MHz (-600KHz, 127.3 PL) and 442.150 MHz (+5MHz, 127.3 PL). All radio operators are invited. Come join us on the air!

MMARC Meetings

Meetings are held every month at 7 p.m. on the Second Thursday in the Training Room of the Cole County Sheriff's Office (350 E. High St, Jefferson City, MO 65101). Map here.

If you wish to take an Amateur Radio License Exam, please contact: Mike Smith (AD0YM) 573-635-8872 ad0ym@arrl.net. Advance arrangements are required.

Meeting Photos

Tech/Builders Night

The first Tuesday of the month 7:00 PM at the Cole County EMS Building 1736 Southridge Dr. Jefferson City, MO. Please come and enjoy the evening with us. If you have anything that you're working on bring it and share with the group.
